What you'll do in this study:

  • You will see an image and a question about it. For example, "How many people are in the image wearing a purple shirt?"
  • You will see two explanations ("Explanation 1" and "Explanation 2") describing how the image was analyzed to answer that question, generated by a chatbot.
  • The explanations may reference the image with a location tag. For example, in the image below, the explanation may say "The person in the purple shirt (Point #1)", and the green point with "1" on it corresponds to the part of the sentence that the explanation refers to.
  • Your job is to answer three questions comparing the two explanations:
    1. Which explanation was easier to understand the process for reaching the answer?
    2. Which explanation made it clearer how the reasoning steps correspond to specific areas in the image?
    3. Which explanation would better help you check if the final answer is correct?

    If both explanations seem equal for a question, select "No difference."
